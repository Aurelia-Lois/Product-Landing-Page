Adobong Manok.
Sabaw, Sarsa, Sarap.

Authentic Filipino Chicken Adobo — slow-braised in vinegar, soy, garlic, and love. The national dish that tastes like home.

Bakit Adobong Manok?

Tender & Juicy

Chicken slow-cooked until fall-off-the-bone, infused with tangy-savory adobo sauce.

Perfect Balance

Vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and black pepper — a timeless flavor symphony.

One-Pot Wonder

Easy to prepare, even better the next day. Freezes like a dream.

Pair with Rice

Best enjoyed with steaming white rice. Spoon that glorious sauce over everything.
